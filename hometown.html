History

Jinan has a long and rich history that dates back over 4,000 years. It has been an important political, economic, and cultural My Hometowncenter since ancient times and has served as the capital of several historical states. Notable historical figures associated with Jinan include Confucius, the famous philosopher and educator, who was born in nearby Qufu.

Population

According to the latest data, Jinan has a population of over 7 million people, with the majority of residents being Han Chinese. Other ethnic groups in the city include Hui, Manchu, and Korean. Mandarin Chinese is the official language spoken in the city.

Economy

Jinan is a major economic center in the region with following fields.

  1. Manufacturing
  2. Agriculture
  3. Service sector
  4. High-tech industries
